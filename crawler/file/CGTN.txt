<h1 class="print"> China's Tianzhou-3 cargo spacecraft docks with space station </h1>
<hr>
<a class="no-print" href=" https://news.cgtn.com/news/2021-09-20/China-s-Tianzhou-3-cargo-spacecraft-docks-with-space-station-13IoaGcTIkM/index.html "> 原文链接 </a> 


<p>China's Tianzhou-3 cargo spacecraft docked with the country's space station's core module Tianhe at 10:08 pm on Monday, 6.5 hours after its launch, according to the China Manned Space Engineering Office.</p> 

<p>The cargo ship docked onto Tianhe's backward facing port using the automatic fast docking process.</p> 

<p>With the Tianzhou-3 docked and Shenzhou-12 headed back to Earth, the space station still consists of three parts: the Tianhe core module, the Tianzhou-2 and the Tianzhou-3.</p> 

<p>Tianzhou-3 is one of the most advanced cargo spacecrafts in the world. It can carry as much as 6.9 tonnes of supplies.</p> 

<p>This mission is part of a series of launches to build China's space station. Soon, three Chinese astronauts, or taikonauts, will board the Shenzhou-13 spacecraft and get into the space station.</p>

<h1 class="print"> China's Rep. to UN sends joint statement on international order </h1>
<hr>
<a class="no-print" href=" https://newsus.cgtn.com/news/2021-09-21/China-s-Rep-to-UN-sends-joint-statement-on-international-order-13J7faJqEXm/index.html "> 原文链接 </a> 


<p>Joint Statement to be Delivered by H.E. Ambassador<br/>Chen Xu at the ID with Independent Expert on<br/>International Order at the HRC48 <br/>(20 September 2021)</p> 

<p>Madam President,</p> 

<p>I have the honor to speak on behalf of a group of countries. A democratic and equitable international order is essential to the promotion and protection of human rights. </p> 

<p>All parties should practice true multilateralism and make the international order more just and equitable, so as to effectively guarantee the full enjoyment of human rights. </p> 

<p>To this end, we should: First, uphold fairness and justice, firmly uphold the UN-centered international system and the international order underpinned by international law, and oppose bullying, unilateralism and double standards. </p> 

<p>Second, pursue win-win cooperation, conduct dialogue and cooperation on the basis of equality and mutual respect, bridge differences through consultations and dialogue, achieve universal security through cooperation and promote common development and prosperity. </p> 

<p>Third, remain open and inclusive, respect the diversity of civilizations and the development paths independently chosen by countries, and refrain from imposing one’s own social system and model on others or creating division and confrontation. </p> 

<p>In the field of human rights, all parties should uphold the principles of universality, impartiality, objectivity and non-selectivity, strengthen solidarity, cooperation and dialogue on an equal footing, and refrain from politicizing human rights issues or interfering in other countries’ internal affairs. </p> 

<p>We call on all States to join hands in defending the common values of humanity, namely peace, development, equity, justice, democracy and freedom, promoting and protecting human rights and building a community with a shared future for human beings.</p> 

<p>Thank you, Madam President.</p> 

<p>China, Cuba, DPRK, Indonesia, Iran, Nicaragua, Russian Federation, Sri Lanka, Syria, Tajikistan, Thailand, Venezuela, Zimbabwe </p>
